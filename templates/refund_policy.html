Refund Policy

Last updated: October 2024

1. Overview

Our refund policy varies depending on the type of course you enroll in. Please read the following details carefully to understand our terms regarding refunds.

2. Self-Paced Programs

Self-paced programs are non-refundable unless canceled by Edufyi Tech Solutions. In such cases, a full refund will be issued to the participant.

3. Mentor-Led or Professional Programs

Refunds for mentor-led or professional programs are provided only if Edufyi Tech Solutions cancels the program. If a program's start is delayed beyond 45 days, participants may request a full refund.

4. Requesting a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us at info@edufyitechsolutions.com within the specified time frame. Make sure to include your course details and reason for the refund in your request.

5. Processing of Refunds

Approved refunds will be processed within 15-20 business days, depending on the payment method used. Please allow additional time for your bank to reflect the refund in your account.
